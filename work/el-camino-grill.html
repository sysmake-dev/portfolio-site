Project Overview

El Camino Grill was developed to explore the design and implementation of a modern one-page website for a local food truck. The project emphasizes presenting essential business information through a clean, responsive interface that allows visitors to quickly learn about the business, browse the menu, locate operating hours, and find contact information. The primary objective was to demonstrate how a simple, well-structured website can effectively support a small local business.

Objectives

  • Develop a responsive one-page website for a hypothetical local food truck.
  • Present essential business information in a clear and accessible layout.
  • Prioritize mobile usability for customers browsing on the go.
  • Create a professional visual identity appropriate for a local business.
  • Demonstrate a maintainable front-end codebase.

Technical Highlights

Mobile-First Design

The website was designed with mobile users as the primary audience, ensuring that navigation, typography, and layout remain clear and accessible across a wide range of screen sizes.

Streamlined Information Architecture

Business information is organized into a single-page layout that allows visitors to quickly locate key details without navigating between multiple pages, creating an efficient and intuitive browsing experience.

Maintainable Front-End Structure

The project demonstrates a clean separation between HTML, CSS, and JavaScript while maintaining a straightforward directory structure that supports future maintenance and expansion.

Challenges

  • Organizing all business information within a single-page layout without overwhelming visitors.
  • Designing a responsive interface that remained readable across different devices.
  • Creating clear visual hierarchy while keeping the design simple and uncluttered.
  • Balancing aesthetics with usability for a local business audience.
  • Establishing a maintainable project structure for future enhancements.

Outcome

The completed project demonstrates how a thoughtfully designed one-page website can provide a professional online presence for a local business. Through responsive design, clear information hierarchy, and a maintainable codebase, the website delivers an accessible and effective customer experience while establishing a strong foundation for future development.
